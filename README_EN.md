## 🧛 The Story
In a world that merges gothic horror with technology, stands **Vania's Castle** — a narrative about an ancestral duty and a resurgent darkness.
After her castle is overrun by evil monsters, protagonist **Vania** decides it's time to reclaim her home. She seeks no glory, only to break a cycle of suffering that has lasted far too long.
---
## 🗡️ Game Stages
### ⚙️ Stage 1: The Entrance
The game begins in the **castle's exterior area**. Vania makes her way to the main entrance and must fight through the first enemies: robotic ravens.
### 🕯️ Stage 2: The Darkness
Vania enters the castle, now **consumed by darkness**. With reduced visibility, the protagonist must unravel unique mechanics until she finally manages to turn the lights back on.
### 💻 Stage 3: The Depths
With the lights now on, Vania ventures through the **castle's depths** in search of the key that unlocks the passage to the invader — where the final confrontation begins.
